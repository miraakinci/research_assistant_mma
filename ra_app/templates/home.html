Frequently Asked Questions

  • Research Assistant Limitations

    The research assistant tool, while robust in handling a variety of tasks, has certain limitations. It is dependent on external APIs such as ACM Digital Library, Semantic Scholar API, and arXiv API, which may be subject to rate limits and occasional downtime, affecting response times.

  • Research Assistant Resources

    The research assistant leverages a range of resources to enhance its functionality. It utilizes well-known academic databases and APIs like the ACM Digital Library, Semantic Scholar API, and arXiv API to access a broad spectrum of research articles and papers based on user queries. Additionally, it employs the Habanero Crossref API to resolve DOIs for papers, ensuring that references are as complete as possible.

  • Research Assistant and AI Safety

    AI safety is a critical consideration in the development and deployment of the research assistant tool. The tool is designed to handle data responsibly, ensuring that personal information is not compromised during web scraping or API interactions. Additionally, the AI models used for summarization are selected with an emphasis on accuracy and reliability to prevent the propagation of misinformation. Ongoing monitoring and updates are part of the tool's maintenance to address any emerging AI safety issues.
